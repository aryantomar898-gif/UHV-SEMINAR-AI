About the Seminar

Why Universal Human Values?

In an age of rapid technological change and globalisation, the need to anchor human progress in timeless values has never been more urgent. MIET Meerut presents this landmark seminar to bring together scholars, students, faculty, and thought leaders from all disciplines under one roof.

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world — but only if it is rooted in values."

This interdisciplinary seminar bridges engineering, science, humanities, management, and the arts to explore how core human values — truth, compassion, justice, and sustainability — can guide personal conduct, professional ethics, and societal transformation.

Conference Themes

Pillars of Discussion

🏛️

Philosophical Foundations

Exploring ancient Indian wisdom, Western ethics, and global philosophical traditions that underpin human values.

⚙️

Values in Technology & Engineering

How ethical design, responsible AI, and sustainable engineering reflect a commitment to universal values.

💼

Ethics in Business & Management

Corporate governance, CSR, and value-based leadership in today's competitive, globalised world.

🌿

Environmental Values & Sustainability

Our moral obligations to the planet — intergenerational justice, climate ethics, and green living.

🎓

Values in Education

NEP 2020 and value-based curricula — preparing students not just for careers, but for life.

🤖

AI, Society & Human Dignity

Navigating the digital age with compassion — privacy, fairness, and the human cost of automation.
